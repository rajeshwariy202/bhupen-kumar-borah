About Bhupen Kumar Borah

A journey of leadership, service, and the people of Assam.

His Roots, His Journey

Born and raised in Pohumora, Assam, Bhupen Kumar Borah’s journey began with grassroots activism during his student days. He served as vice-president of his college student union and general secretary of Dibrugarh University’s postgraduate students’ union, eventually taking on leadership roles in the Youth Congress.

“My vision for Assam has always focused on building strong, self-reliant villages and empowering youth to lead from the front.”

With a Master's degree in Political Science, Borah has been a vocal advocate for rural development and local self-governance in Assam. His leadership emphasizes empowering young people, strengthening communities, and driving inclusive progress.

Political Journey Timeline

Engaged in party outreach activities across the state, including the "Sutale Sutale Congress" campaign to reconnect with grassroots communities.
Became the president of Assam Pradesh Congress Committee to lead organizational efforts in the state.
Represented the party as a candidate for the Tezpur constituency in the 2014 general elections.
Elected as the MLA from Bihpuria, marking the beginning of a notable political journey.
